About Us

Paradise Bakery and Cafe is a fast-casual bakery-cafe headquartered in St. Louis, MO and owned by Panera Bread Company. The company was founded in Long Beach, Calif. in 1976 and currently operates more than 50 company-owned and franchised locations in seven states. All menu items are prepared daily with the finest ingredients available offering freshly baked cookies, muffins, brownies, croissants, quiches, breads, soups, salads and sandwiches.
